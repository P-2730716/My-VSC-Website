Past Work

I have had an interest within the area of photography since the age of around 12, but after school work & other effecting factors, I was finally able to begin paid photography work from the age of 18. My first job ended up being based in the primary school that my mom worked for & I was given 5 reuirements for my work, which proved to not only myself, but also the customer that I can still have good-quality work even if I am given restrictions. Those restrictions were to have images resemble a word - one of such being perseverance - whilst still having a natural & unforced feel to the image itself. Not only were the pictures I took used for the website, some were also framed & put up around the school; which without blowing my own trumpet, means that the images that I took were of a high-quality & also therefore satisfied the customer.

After finding out that it wasn't simply just an interest, but rather something I could do full-time, I decided to book another job. rather than working in a school, this time it was for a football-coaching business & so I had to get images of a plethora of people/ activities/ items. Similar to last time, I was given some requirements - this time a bit more simple, but the challenging part was that the job was over two days. Normally, & for established photographers this would not be a problem, but to be completely trasparent with you it did challenge me because I was just starting out at this point & so spending a long period of time constantly doing the same thing seemed boring, but I stood-up to the challenge, did not get complacent withing the process of achieving a high-quality image & completed the job to the best of my ability.

About Me

Firstly, my name is Carrick Hasketh-Boston & at the time of creating this, I am 19 years old. On the 20th of January 2004 I was happily born into a beautiful family with my parents & older brother, then soon after myself a younger sister. Life didn's start off fantastically however, as when I first arrived home my clever 1-year-old-brother at the time decided to clonk me around the head with an empty milk bottle - which is probably a valid reason as to why I have ended up the way that I am. After going through primary school & passing my SAT's (yes I am clever enough to pass those) I began to attend Staffordshire-based secondary school 'The Hart School' in 2015. This is where my passion for media/ photography began, as throughout secondary school, I had the chance of visiting places like Barcelona, Cadiz, Paris, Brugge, California & many other places. Originally, it began as just an easy & accessible way of storing memories, but I feel like the older I grew, the more that I appreciated littler things - such as architecture & culture - & so wanted those memories to give whoever sees them the same feeling I felt when I did. Anyway, after high-school I attended a local sixth-form & this is where i began my journey on photography & media in an academic sense. However, because of the effects of covid, having to isolate & personal issues, I did fall out of love with & lose the spur to put effort into my school work - as did so many others at such a hard time, as we are only human. I still managed to achieve a grade good enough to get accepted into my university Of Choice, that being De Montfort University, & so through many trials & tribulations, I still managed to do as good as my ability would let me. & now i am here. Although I havent reached my final goal of financial stability & a full, quality photography portfolio, I feel as if I am definitely on a clearer pathway to not only finding my passion for photography & spur to complete my work, but also on a path towards achieving my goals as well.
